TED Guidelines & Rules

1
Time Limit and Content Purity

Talks must be under 18 minutes and free from promoting products, services, political agendas, or religious beliefs.

18:00
2
Originality and Honesty

Speakers must present original, honest content, with no plagiarism or sponsorship influence.

3
Production Quality and Community Effort

TEDx events require professional production quality and should be managed by volunteers committed to community-driven efforts.

4
Diversity and Inclusivity

Diversity, inclusivity, and respect are core values for both the speakers and the audience, ensuring a wide range of ideas and backgrounds.

5
Curation and Fact-Checking

Talks should go through a strict curation process to ensure they align with TEDx standards, including fact-checking for accuracy.

Fact-checking in progress...

6
Global Reach, Local Focus

While TEDx is global, TEDx events must focus on ideas relevant to the local community.

7
Sponsorship Without Influence

Sponsors can support the event financially but cannot influence the content or speaker selection.
